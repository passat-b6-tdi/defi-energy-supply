# Team

DefiEnergySupply is built by Bohdan Pukhno, a Web3 / smart contract developer
with 5+ years of experience in Solidity and DeFi. He holds a B.Sc. in
Electronic Engineering (2023) and an M.Sc. in Electrical, Electronics and
Communications Engineering (2025) from the National Technical University of
Ukraine "Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute," and is currently
completing a Ph.D. in the same field.

His research and this project trace back to **FORWARDER** ("FOrtifying
SmaRtgrids With BlockchAin-Powered CybeRsecurity for Decentralized Energy
Accounting and TRansactions"), an international research project on the
same core question — automated, quality-aware electricity settlement in
Smart Grid / Microgrid systems — funded by **CRDF Global** via the Kyiv
Polytechnic Science Park. That funding built the technical foundation
DefiEnergySupply runs on today: the settlement contracts, the subgraph, and
the Prometheus/Grafana monitoring pipeline, validated on a physical
laboratory stand (solar panel, battery storage, smart meters) before the
system moved to its current Base Sepolia deployment.

The work is documented in a peer-reviewed publication: Yamnenko I.,
Khokhlov Y., Pukhno B., *"Blockchain for Automation of Mutual Settlements
for Generated and Consumed Electricity in Smartgrid Systems,"* Springer
Nature Switzerland, 2025
([DOI: 10.1007/978-3-032-02272-1](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-032-02272-1)).
