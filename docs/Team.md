# Team

DefiEnergySupply is built by Bohdan Pukhno, a Web3 / smart contract developer
with 5+ years of experience in Solidity and DeFi. He holds a B.Sc. in
Electronic Engineering (2023) and an M.Sc. in Electrical, Electronics and
Communications Engineering (2025) from the National Technical University of
Ukraine "Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute," and is currently
completing a Ph.D. in the same field.

His research and this project share the same core question — how to make
electricity settlement in Microgrid / Smart Grid systems automated and
quality-aware — documented in a peer-reviewed publication:
*"Blockchain-Based Electricity Settlement in Smart Grid Systems"* (Springer,
September 2025).
